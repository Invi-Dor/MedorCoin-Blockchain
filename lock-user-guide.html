What is Liquidity Lock

Liquidity Lock is a service provided by MedorCoin that temporarily restricts access to tokens, contracts, or other digital assets. This ensures security, builds investor confidence, and integrates seamlessly with the MedorCoin ecosystem. By locking your liquidity, you demonstrate long-term commitment and enhance trust in your project. To start, create an account.

Advantages of Locking Your Crypto Liquidity

  • Increased investor confidence
  • Reduced risk of sudden token dumps
  • Secure management of smart contracts
  • Absolute control over timing and access

Example

A developer locks a portion of tokens for six months. Investors see this on their dashboard, assuring them that the project team is committed to long-term stability.

How to Lock Your Liquidity on MedorCoin

MedorCoin provides multiple options for locking your digital assets. Each type offers unique security features and benefits. You can connect wallet to begin.

Lock Your Tokens

Locking tokens ensures they are temporarily inaccessible. This prevents sudden sales and demonstrates commitment to investors.

  • Customisable lock duration

Lock Your NFTs

Locking NFTs secures your digital collectibles or project assets while retaining full ownership.

  • Ideal for marketplace listings or collaborations
  • Access control for specific users

Lock Your Launch Pool

Ensure transparency and fairness during fundraising by locking launch pools.

  • Prevents premature withdrawals

Who Can Lock Assets

For Developers

Developers can lock tokens, NFTs, contracts, or launch pools. This demonstrates commitment to investors, showing that project assets are secure and cannot be accessed prematurely. MedorCoin provides full technical support 24/7.

For Investors

Investors can lock their assets to safeguard investments, prevent losses, and ensure long-term participation in projects they trust. Locked assets are visible on the dashboard, providing transparency and reassurance.

FAQs

What is the duration for locking assets?
You can choose any duration when locking assets, from a few days to several years, depending on your requirements.
Can I unlock assets before the lock period ends?
No, assets remain inaccessible until the lock period expires to ensure security and investor trust.
Are locked assets still visible to investors?
Yes, investors can see locked assets on their dashboard, providing full transparency.
Is locking reversible?
No, once an asset is locked, it cannot be reversed until the end of the lock period.
Who can lock NFTs?
Developers and investors can lock NFTs to secure ownership or project assets.
Does MedorCoin provide technical support?
Yes, MedorCoin provides 24/7 support for all liquidity locking services.
What makes MedorCoin different?
MedorCoin integrates locking services within its ecosystem with secure audited smart contracts and full control for users.
Can contracts be locked?
Yes, developers can lock contract addresses to prevent unauthorised modifications.
Is it safe to lock launch pools?
Yes, locking launch pools prevents premature withdrawals and ensures fairness during fundraising.
Do investors need technical knowledge to lock assets?
No, MedorCoin provides an intuitive interface and support, making locking easy for all users.
