MedorCoin Wallet — Create Wallet

1) Generate Wallet

This will create a Bitcoin-like Base58Check address (P2PKH) from a locally-generated mnemonic.

Click address to copy
2) Recovery Phrase

Your 12-word recovery phrase is the secret to your wallet. Reveal only when you are ready to securely store it offline.

Important: Do not share this phrase. If you lose it, you lose access to funds permanently. Save a physical copy offline.
Encrypted backup will download after you enter a password.
3) Send Funds (Test)

Send funds from your new wallet to a recipient address. This is a client-side demo; no real network call is made here.

Security & Privacy:

This wallet is client-side. Recovery phrases and private keys never leave your device. Keep backups offline in a secure location. Losing the recovery phrase means permanent loss of access to funds.
