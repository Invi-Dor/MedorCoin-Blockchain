How Mining Works (Step-by-Step)

  1. Choose a plan: Free tier or paid plans with different GH/s and durations.
  2. Provide payment method: Debit/Credit card bridge to crypto or crypto direct (BNB, ETH, BTC).
  3. For card payments: USD amount is converted to the chosen crypto at current rate inside the app.
  4. For crypto payments: send the exact address to the platform for the selected plan’s duration and speed.
  5. Contracts renew every 24 hours on paid plans; free tier renewals occur every 12 hours with limited GH/s.
  6. Earn MedorCoin as you mine; higher speed yields more rewards.
  7. Before starting, answer two quick quizzes and complete a verification (email or phone).
  8. All accounts require adult consent (18+) and acceptance of terms.
