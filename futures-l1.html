1. How to Read Simple Binance Analysis Before Trading

Before trading, understand basic market analysis from Binance:

  • Price Action & Trend: Identify bullish or bearish movement.
  • Support & Resistance: Know where price stalls or reverses.
  • Market Sentiment: Use RSI, MACD, volume to judge strength.
  • Timeframes & Moving Averages: Use multiple charts for better timing.
