Futures Level 1 — Crypto Futures Trading Fundamentals

1) Margin & 2) Settlement

Margin: The money you put down to open a leveraged trade.

Settlement: The asset you receive your profit or loss in when closing.

3) Stablecoins & 4) USD‑M

Stablecoins: Cryptos designed to keep stable value (ex: USDT).

USD‑M: A type where margin & settlement are in USD‑linked coins.
