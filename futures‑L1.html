Futures Level 1 — Crypto Futures Trading Fundamentals

1) Margin & 2) Settlement

Margin: The money you put down to open a leveraged trade. It acts as a security deposit.

Settlement: The asset you receive your profit or loss in when the trade is closed.

3) Stablecoins & 4) USD-M

Stablecoins: Cryptocurrencies designed to keep a stable value, usually linked to a real currency like the US dollar (Example: USDT).

USD-M: A type of futures contract where margin and settlement are in US dollar‑linked stablecoins like USDT.
