What Is Trading

Trading means buying and selling an asset to profit from price changes. In crypto this means you do not hold the coin forever. You aim to buy at a low price and sell at a higher price. For MedorCoin traders watch the price movements on an exchange and enter trades when they expect the price to rise or fall.

üìä Five Essential Trading Terms

Market Order

A market order is an instruction to buy or sell immediately at the best current price. It prioritizes execution over price control so you enter or exit trades fast.

Example: You think MedorCoin will rise quickly so you place a market buy to get in right away.

Bid and Ask

Bid is the highest price a buyer is willing to pay for an asset.
Ask is the lowest price a seller accepts.
The spread is the difference between them. A smaller spread usually means cheaper and faster trades.

Bull Market and Bear Market

Bullish means prices are generally rising.
Bearish means prices are generally falling.

Knowing this helps you choose a strategy. Buy in a bull market and consider selling or being cautious in a bear market.

Stop Loss Order

A stop loss automatically closes your trade if the price moves against you to a set level. It is essential for limiting losses.

Example: If you buy MedorCoin at $1 and set a stop loss at $0.90, your trade will close automatically if the price falls to $0.90.

Leverage

Leverage means using borrowed money to control a larger position than your own capital allows. It can make profits bigger but also increases losses.

Beginners should be cautious because high leverage can lead to quick losses.
