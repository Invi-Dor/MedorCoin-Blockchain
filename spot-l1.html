What Is Trading

Trading means buying and selling an asset to profit from price changes. In crypto this means you do not hold the coin forever. You aim to buy at a low price and sell at a higher price. For MedorCoin traders watch the price movements on an exchange and enter trades when they expect the price to rise or fall.

ğŸ“Š Five Essential Trading Terms

Market Order: A market order is an instruction to buy or sell immediately at the best current price.

Bid and Ask: Bid is the highest price a buyer will pay. Ask is the lowest a seller will accept.

Bull Market and Bear Market: Bullish = rising prices, Bearish = falling prices.

Stop Loss Order: Automatically closes your trade at a set level to limit losses.

Leverage: Using borrowed money to increase position size; can amplify gains and losses.
