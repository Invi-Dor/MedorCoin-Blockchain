Medorcoin Hub Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

This policy governs the Medorcoin Node (172.235.50.31), Developer API, Mining Academy, and DeFi Launchpool services.

1. Data Collection by Service

  • Blockchain Node & RPC: We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). Our RPC temporarily processes network metadata to facilitate 80-byte header synchronization.
  • Developer API (BaaS): We store request metadata to maintain service stability and prevent unauthorized access to our infrastructure.
  • Mining Academy: Registration for courses may involve collecting basic contact information (email/name) used solely for curriculum delivery and student support.
  • Liquidity & Launchpool: All interactions with liquidity locking or launchpool smart contracts are recorded on the public ledger. On-chain data is immutable and transparent to all participants.

2. Data Security & Storage

We implement industry-standard encryption and access controls to safeguard your data against unauthorized alteration or loss. Your data is stored on our secure infrastructure in Los Angeles, US.

3. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected (e.g., for the duration of your course enrollment). On-chain transaction records are permanent.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data held in our Mining Academy or API databases.

5. Compliance

We comply with applicable global privacy standards (GDPR/CCPA) and cooperate with legal inquiries where required by law.
